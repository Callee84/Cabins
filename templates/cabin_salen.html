Welcome to Hattvägen, Sälen!

Hattvägen

Located in Linvallen, one of the most popular ski resorts in Sweden. Only 3 minute drive from the 31 slopes that Linvallen has to offer.
The cabin is optimized for large parties with its fireplace in the middle of the room and the long walnut table for cozy dinners. Atmospheric logs adorn the roof and the total area of ​​214 square meters. Many have celebrated holidays such as Christmas, New Year and Easter here, but also the occasional 50th anniversary celebration and wedding. The cabin naturally has ski-in/ski-out mode and about 1 km from the nearest cross-country track.


The cabin is equipped with 5 beds, distributed over four bedrooms. For larger groups, there is the option of extra beds. The living room has an open floor plan. The bathrooms are fully tiled with a shower, sauna and combined washing machine and dryer and wood burning stove.

  • Bedrooms: 5
  • Beds: 8-12
  • Distance to ski lift: Ski-in Ski-out
