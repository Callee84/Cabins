Welcome to Öland!

Kolstad, Öland

Want some peace and quiet but with the possibility of some party and? Located only 10 minutes from Borgholm and the beautiful beaches of Köpingsvik. Holiday home built in 2018, with open floor plan and generous spaces. Large lovely terrace where you can hang out, sunbathe, grill and have a good time. Here you also have fantastic evening sun that you can enjoy. Follow the road straight down 500 m and you will come to Kalmarsund, which offers Öland's absolute best sandy beaches with a lovely shallow swimming beach that is suitable for families with children. In Köpingsvik there is a patisserie, grocery store and some smaller shops, as well as some restaurants and pubs. There are also a couple of very nice beach restaurants with entertainment and after beach every evening during the summer. Everything within walking distance from the house


The cabin is equipped with 6 beds, distributed over five bedrooms. For larger groups, there is the option of extra beds. The living room has an open floor plan. The bathrooms are fully tiled with a showe, and combined washing machine and dryer and wood burning stove. The lovely terrace invites you to late night grilling and entertaing large partys.
